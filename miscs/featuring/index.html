News

Some news and online articles featuring me.

  • 2024/04/10: I received the Distinguished Service Award for the UWaterloo’s Women in Mathematics Directed Reading Program. During my time here, I coordinated three projects and mentored four students.
  • 2024/03/01: My paper: General Additive Network Effect Model was featured in the “Design and Analysis of Experiments for Data Science” webinar by the New England Journal of Statistics in Data Science.
  • 2023/11/14: I appeared on the news of the Statistical Consulting and Survey Research Unit for my collaborative paper on tax fairness and tax compliance.
  • 2023/05/30: I won the Student Research Presentation Awards in Business and Industrial Statistics and the First Prize in Case Studies in Data Analysis Poster Competition at the SSC Annual Meeting, 2023.
  • 2023/03/02: I featured in the 2023 CANSSI International Day of Women and Girls in Science.
  • 2016/12/15: I won the KAIST Best Undergraduate Thesis Award with my undergraduate thesis “Flash Crash: An investigation by Multi-Agent Simulations”.
  • I served as Vice President of VN.KAIST for two years.
  • 2012/05/18: My team won ISEF 2012 First Prize in Mechanical and Electrical Engineering. We are the first Vietnamese team to win a grand award at ISEF.
